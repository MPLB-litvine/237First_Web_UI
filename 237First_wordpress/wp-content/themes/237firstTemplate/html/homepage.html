Welcome to the world's biggest music network.

237First is a music networking platform. We focus on building a network for our users through which they can stream, download, share, invest, market and promote Cameroon music. Our users can either join preexisting networks or start their own. You can stream your favorite songs, store them online and share them to millions of people with just a click of a button. Registering to our platform

As a registered member of 237First you can stream (listen to) music, download, share on Facebook, Tweet and Join A Network (JAN). Joining a network unlocks more features which are described in the JAN section.


Registering to our platform is open and free for all. Registration is easy and straightforward. Simply click on the menu icon on the top right corner of our page and select SIGN UP. Fill a short form and you will be automatically redirected to our homepage as a registered user.

Join A Network (JAN)

Join other music lovers and share your taste with your audience. You automatically become a "marketing agent" for the person who invited you into a network and vice versa. However, if both users belong to each others networks then they become "partners" and therefore can share on both their networks.
